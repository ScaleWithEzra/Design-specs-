Clean & Modern

A calm, premium, native-feeling system. Monochrome neutral chrome, one green accent, depth from contrast and a whisper of shadow, big quiet numerals, and a single signature bounce. Everything below is the real thing — same tokens, same components as the prototype.

Elevation

Depth comes from contrast first, then a whisper of shadow. Cards get a soft two-layer ambient lift; floating chrome goes deeper and pairs with a frosted blur. Never a hard or dark drop shadow.

Command Dock

One floating surface that fluidly switches roles instead of a static tab bar. Collapsed it's [ home ] [ Ask anything… ] [ + ] — AI always one tap away. Tap home and it morphs: the input rises and a full tab row unfolds beneath it, without ever losing the AI input. It's a layout morph, never a slide.

Action Grid

Tapping + doesn't open a plain menu — it expands the dock into the nav, flips + to ×, and floats a frosted panel of circular quick-actions in a 3-column grid, with the green AI avatar as the center tile. Items pop in staggered on the bounce. It reads as a command center, not a dropdown.

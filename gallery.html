Style 03 · Warm editorial

Set in warm type, printed on paper.

Atelier is the counterweight to Clean & Modern. Where that style is cool, monochrome and soft-edged, this one is warm cream, a serif display, and hairline rules — the feel of a well-set magazine rather than a dashboard. One clay accent, generous margins, and a calm ink-settle motion. Built by harvesting Park UI primitives and re-theming them to these tokens.

The signature composition

An interface that reads like a page.

The feature card is where Atelier states itself: a confident serif headline, a single warm image field, body in Inter, and a byline ruled off below. No drop shadow — the border and the contrast carry it. This is the unit a product built in Atelier composes its surfaces from.
